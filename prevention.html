Prevention


Source:

AllMed

Exercise

High levels of physical activity reduces the risk of heart disease by 25%. It can improve cholesterol and blood pressure. It also helps in maintaining a healthy weight.

Healthy diet

Diets high in fruits and vegetables decreases the risk of cardiovascular diseases. Decreasing your cholesterol levels will also prevent heart disease.

Smoking cessation

Quit smoking and prevent from getting secondhand smoke.

Maintaining a healthy weight

Physical activity and healthy diets can help maintain a healthy weight or lose weight.

Reduce stress

Stress can affect your heart's health, and keeping your stress low will help prevent heart disease.
